About my Journey

Software Development

I started my programming journey at Vanier College in October 2022 to this day, worked with many programming languages like Java and C# , as well as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, worked with databases and SQL, Linux, and Git, and much more tools.
Still learning to this day as this field keeps expanding, my aim is to master this craft as much as I love doing it.

Graphic Design

In 2016 I started learning graphic design and photo editing on my own, after a while I became a freelancer and worked on a lot of projects since then, until I stopped in 2019 when I came to Canada.
With many years of experience with Adobe Creative Cloud such as Photoshop and Illustrator, I loved designing so much that's why I am so interested in designing and creating websites as a front-end developer, learning a new tool, a new thing every day.

Video Editing

My video editing journey was pretty much alongside learning Graphic Design. 5 years of experience in video editing, with powerful tools such as Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and Davinci Resolve.
Worked on many projects most of which are still used and online to this day, learned a lot during this journey, video editing needs patience and creativity, still practicing video editing to this day.

E-commerce

My story with E-commerce is a long and ambitious story, I started to learn E-commerce back in 2017 and I have launched since then a lot of online stores and tried a lot of different types of E-commerce, some succeeded, many failed, but I've picked up a few skills along the way, I've learned about SEO, marketing, Facebook advertising management, and Shopify.
It was an interesting adventure I would say.
